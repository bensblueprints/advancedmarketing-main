Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Advanced Marketing Limited ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website advancedmarketing.co and use our services, including our SMS messaging program.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and business information you voluntarily provide when contacting us, booking a consultation, subscribing to our newsletter, or using our services.
  • SMS Opt-In Data: When you opt in to receive SMS messages, we collect your mobile phone number, your consent status, the date and time of opt-in, and the source of opt-in (e.g., website popup, form).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (including localStorage) to enhance your experience, remember your preferences (such as popup dismissal), and analyze site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. We may also use third-party analytics tools that collect aggregated usage data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests
  • Send you marketing emails (with your consent)
  • Send you SMS/MMS messages including service alerts and promotional offers (with your express written consent)
  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

SMS/Text Messaging Program

When you opt in to our SMS program, you agree to receive recurring automated marketing and informational text messages from Benjamin Boyce / Advanced Marketing (including service alerts and promotional messages) at the mobile number you provide. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any purchase. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at ben@advancedmarketing.co.

Mobile Information Sharing: We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, lease, or otherwise transfer for profit any phone numbers or personal information collected through our SMS program to any third party. We will not share your mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All information collected from the SMS program will be used strictly for the purpose of delivering the messages you have consented to receive.

Data Collected via SMS: We collect your mobile phone number, opt-in consent records, message delivery and response data, and opt-out requests. This data is stored securely and retained only as long as you remain opted in, plus a reasonable period thereafter for compliance purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services (including SMS message delivery), subject to confidentiality agreements. These providers are bound to use your information only to perform services on our behalf. We may also disclose information when required by law.

Data Security & Handling

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS), secure storage of consent records, and restricted access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. SMS opt-in records are retained for the duration of your subscription plus a compliance retention period.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for email: click unsubscribe; for SMS: reply STOP)
  • Request data portability
  • Request a copy of your opt-in consent records

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ben@advancedmarketing.co.
